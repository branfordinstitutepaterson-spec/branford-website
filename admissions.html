Admission Requirements

What you need to get started

Medical student applicants at Alent Institute must meet the following requirements to be considered for enrollment in our healthcare training programs:

  • Possess a High School Diploma or General Education Certificate (G.E.D.)
  • Alternatively, pass the TABE Test (a 9th grade reading and writing assessment)
  • Complete an admissions interview with our enrollment team
  • Be at least 18 years of age (or 16 with parental consent)
  • Provide a valid government-issued photo ID
  • Submit a completed application form

Note: Some programs may have additional prerequisites. Please contact our admissions team for program-specific requirements.

Enrollment Process

Four simple steps to start your healthcare career

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit your application form online or visit one of our campuses in person to apply.

2

Meet Requirements

Provide your high school diploma, G.E.D., or schedule your TABE test at our campus.

3

Admissions Interview

Meet with our admissions team to discuss your goals and select the right program for you.

4

Enroll & Begin

Complete enrollment, arrange payment or financial support, and start classes on the next available date.
