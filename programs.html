Our Programs

Comprehensive healthcare training programs to launch your medical career

Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)

Alent Institute provides certified nursing assistant training in NJ for those working towards a State CNA License. Our CNA program gives students the foundational skills and knowledge needed to provide quality patient care in hospitals, nursing homes, and long-term care facilities.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic patient care and daily living assistance
  • Vital signs monitoring and documentation
  • Infection control and safety procedures
  • Communication with patients and healthcare team
  • Patient rights and ethical responsibilities
  • Clinical skills practice in laboratory settings
Duration: 4 Weeks
Schedule: Day, Evening & Weekend
Certification: State CNA License

Certified Medical Assistant

Our comprehensive certified medical assistant program prepares students for a dynamic career in healthcare. All Alent Institute graduates receive a guaranteed medical assistant internship position. Medical assistant students will learn important and marketable skills across multiple disciplines.

What You Will Learn

  • Phlebotomy skills and blood collection techniques
  • Electrocardiography (EKG) procedures
  • Pharmacy Technician fundamentals
  • Patient clinical procedures
  • Medical receptionist responsibilities
  • Medical assisting and administrative duties
  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) management
Duration: 20 Weeks
Schedule: Day & Evening
Includes: Guaranteed Internship

EKG/Phlebotomy Technician

The EKG Technician Program prepares students to become skilled electrocardiograph technicians. Most graduates go on to study Phlebotomy and become Combo EKG/Phlebotomy Technicians, making them highly versatile and employable in the healthcare industry.

What You Will Learn

  • 12-lead EKG application and interpretation
  • Cardiac anatomy and physiology
  • Venipuncture and capillary puncture techniques
  • Blood specimen collection and handling
  • Laboratory safety and infection control
  • Medical terminology related to cardiology
Duration: 6 Weeks (EKG) + 6 Weeks (Phlebotomy)
Schedule: Day & Evening
Certification: EKG & Phlebotomy Technician

Patient Care Technician

The Patient Care Technician program is a multifaceted skills training program that combines our Certified Nursing Assistant and EKG/Phlebotomy programs. Graduates are prepared for a wide range of patient care roles in various healthcare settings.

What You Will Learn

  • All CNA competencies and clinical skills
  • EKG application and monitoring
  • Phlebotomy and blood specimen collection
  • Advanced patient care techniques
  • Medical documentation and charting
  • Team collaboration in healthcare environments
Duration: 16 Weeks
Schedule: Day & Evening
Certification: CNA + EKG/Phlebotomy

Medical Billing & Coding

Learn the essential skills for a career in medical billing, coding, and electronic health records management. This program trains students to accurately code diagnoses and procedures, process insurance claims, and manage patient billing in healthcare settings.

What You Will Learn

  • ICD-10 and CPT coding systems
  • Medical terminology and anatomy
  • Health insurance and claims processing
  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) systems
  • Medical office administration
  • HIPAA compliance and regulations
Duration: 12 Weeks
Schedule: Day & Evening
Certification: Medical Billing Specialist

Certified Home Health Aide

Become a Certified Home Health Aide and provide essential in-home care to patients who need assistance with daily living activities. This program prepares students to deliver compassionate, quality care in patients' homes.

What You Will Learn

  • Personal care and hygiene assistance
  • Nutrition and meal preparation
  • Vital signs measurement
  • Home safety and emergency procedures
  • Communication with patients and families
  • Documentation and reporting
Duration: 2 Weeks
Schedule: Day & Weekend
Certification: CHHA Certification

Dialysis Technician

Our Dialysis Technician program trains BONENT certified technicians with the knowledge and skills needed to perform dialysis procedures. Graduates are prepared for rewarding careers in dialysis centers and hospitals.

What You Will Learn

  • Hemodialysis procedures and equipment operation
  • Renal anatomy and physiology
  • Water treatment and dialysate preparation
  • Patient assessment and monitoring
  • Infection control in dialysis settings
  • Emergency procedures and troubleshooting
Duration: 12 Weeks
Schedule: Day & Evening
Certification: BONENT Certification

Certified Medication Aide

The Certified Medication Aide program trains students to safely administer medications to patients under the supervision of licensed nurses. This certification enhances the skillset of CNAs and other healthcare workers.

What You Will Learn

  • Medication administration techniques
  • Pharmacology fundamentals
  • Drug classifications and interactions
  • Documentation and record keeping
  • Patient safety protocols
  • Regulatory compliance and legal responsibilities
Duration: 4 Weeks
Schedule: Day & Evening
Certification: Certified Medication Aide

CPR/BLS Monthly Classes

Alent Institute offers CPR/BLS classes for healthcare professionals every month. By completing our AED, Basic Life Support, and AHA CPR classes, students receive a two-year AHA CPR certification. These classes are open to both current students and outside healthcare professionals.

What You Will Learn

  • Adult, child, and infant CPR techniques
  • Automated External Defibrillator (AED) use
  • Basic Life Support (BLS) protocols
  • Choking relief procedures
  • Team-based resuscitation scenarios
Duration: 1 Day
Schedule: Monthly Sessions
Certification: 2-Year AHA CPR Certification
